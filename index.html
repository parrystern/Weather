Instructions

  1. Boil the meat In a pot, add the meat and water. Boil until tender.
  2. Prepare the palm nut cream In a separate bowl, mix the palm nut cream with water to form a smooth paste.
  3. Strain the palm nut cream Strain the palm nut cream to remove any lumps.
  4. Add the palm nut cream Pour the palm nut cream into the pot with the boiled meat.
  5. Add the fish Add the fish to the pot and let it cook for a few minutes.
  6. Add the spices Add the ginger, garlic, onions, and pepper to the pot.
  7. Season the soup Add salt and seasoning to taste.
  8. Simmer Let the soup simmer for about 30 minutes.
  9. Add vegetables Add the vegetables and let them cook for a few minutes.
  10. Serve Serve the soup hot with rice or fufu.
